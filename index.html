Charles Arnal

Charles Arnal

I am a postdoctoral researcher at Meta, working on reasoning in Large Language Models.
Before that, I completed a first postdoc in Topological Data Analysis at the Laboratoire de Mathématiques d'Orsay, Paris-Saclay, and a PhD under the supervision of Ilia Itenberg, at the IMJ-PRG, Sorbonne Université.
My research interests include Machine Learning in general and Large Language Models in particular, as well as Topological Data Analysis, Real Algebraic Geometry, and any topic at the intersection of Geometry and Statistics.
